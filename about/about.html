About Arigit Singh

Arijit Singh is a renowned Indian playback singer known for his soulful and emotionally resonant voice, especially in romantic songs. Born on 25th April 1987 in Jiaganj, Murshidabad, West Bengal, Arijit gained initial recognition as one of the top six contestants in the reality singing show Fame Gurukul in 2005. Although he did not win, he captured the attention of both the audience and the music industry. He later won another reality show titled 10 Ke 10 Le Gaye Dil, which was based on audience votes. His professional journey began when he worked as an assistant to music composer Pritam, and he gradually carved a niche for himself in Bollywood.
